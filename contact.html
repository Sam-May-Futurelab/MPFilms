Frequently Asked Questions

Can I apply the films myself?

Yes, most of our films are designed for easy application. We provide comprehensive application kits that include a squeegee, film cutters, and core handles to help you achieve professional results. For larger projects or complex applications, we recommend consulting with our team first for guidance.

How long does the UV protection last?

UV protection varies by product. Our standard films offer protection for up to 1 month, while our premium products like Hullguard Premium (MPF701) provide industry-leading UV resistance for up to 12 months. The specific weatherability information is included in each product description.

Do you ship outside the UK?

Yes, we ship internationally to most countries. Shipping times and costs vary based on location and order size. For specific information about shipping to your region, please contact our sales team who can provide you with a detailed quote.

Are all your products recyclable?

Most of our products are fully recyclable. We work closely with Nitto, who is committed to developing environmentally friendly products. Each product page indicates whether the specific film or tape is recyclable. We're constantly working to expand our eco-conscious options.
