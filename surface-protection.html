Surface Protection on Vessel ("SPV")

We offer all of these Hullguard and Slipguard surface protection products, from the Nitto SPV range, on lightweight reels for ease of handling or in custom sizes up to 1.5m wide for some products.

Application Information

Proper Application For Best Results

  • 1

    Surface Preparation

    Ensure all surfaces are clean, dry and dust-free before application. Any contaminants will affect adhesion.

  • 2

    Temperature Conditions

    Apply films at temperatures between 10°C and 35°C for optimal adhesion properties.

  • 3

    Handling

    Use 2-inch tension control core handles for easier application, especially on angled surfaces.

  • 4

    Smoothing

    Use the MPF squeegee to remove air bubbles and ensure proper adhesion to the surface.
